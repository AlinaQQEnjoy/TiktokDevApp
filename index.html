Manage, schedule, and publish short-form video content.

Short-Form Video Manager helps creators and small teams organize video posts, prepare publishing schedules, and manage content workflows from a single web dashboard.

What the app does

Content Management

Keep video drafts, captions, publishing notes, and status details in one organized workspace.

Scheduling

Plan upcoming short-form video posts and review what is ready to publish.

Publishing Workflow

Support authorized publishing workflows for connected social media accounts.

Platform

This service is currently provided as a web application. Users access the app in a browser and connect only the accounts they authorize.
